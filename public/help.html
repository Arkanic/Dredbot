Q: The bot isn't responding to anything that I send

A: The bot probably doesn't have enough permissions to send a message, try giving it a role that allows it to send messages inside of that particular channel.

Q: The bot still isn't responding...

A: The bot might be offline, which it has no reason to be. This is a problem on my part.

Q: The bot responds with "Caching" when i send it a command

A: The bot is currently updating its ship list. this should take ~half a minute, so sit tight.
